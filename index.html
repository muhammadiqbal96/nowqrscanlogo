Section 01

What is NowQR? — The Simple Explanation

NowQR is a digital marketing tool for small business owners. It lets them create two things very quickly:

🎨 A Beautiful Ad Page
  • A visually designed promotional page — like a digital flyer or poster
  • Created using a Canva-style drag-and-drop editor
  • AI writes the text automatically based on the business info
  • Has NO phone numbers, emails, or website links on it
  • Hosted on our domain (e.g., give.nowqr.com/theirbusiness)
📲 An Animated ScanLogo Button
  • A branded, animated QR code button — called a "ScanLogo"
  • Shaped like a logo, shield, wheel, eye, etc. — and it moves/spins
  • Can be scanned with a phone OR clicked on a screen
  • Links to wherever the customer wants (their store, PayPal, YouTube)
  • Dynamic — the destination can be changed without reprinting

The ad page grabs attention. The ScanLogo is the only button on it. One tap — and the customer lands wherever the business owner wants them to go.

Section 02

What NowQR Does — and Does NOT Do

This is important. The client confirmed this on Feb 22, 2026:

"We do not create customers' action pages. We have a Canva page creator. The Canva page is created by AI... the QR code button comes from premade action image buttons... the customer must upload their text and destination webpage... the Canva page cannot feature any websites or phone numbers or email address... the ScanLogo is the only clickable part of the Canva page."

What NowQR Provides What the Customer Provides Status
Canva-style ad page editor Their text, images, business name, logo ✓ Confirmed
AI to write headlines & copy Basic info about their business & goal ✓ Confirmed
Pre-made animated ScanLogo button styles Their destination URL (where they want to send people) ✓ Confirmed
Hosting for their ad page on our domain Nothing — we host it for them ✓ Confirmed
Their actual sales page / website Customer owns and manages their own website ✗ Not our job
Any contact info displayed on the ad page Not allowed — ScanLogo is the only CTA ✗ Not allowed
⚠️ Key Rule

The Canva ad page must NEVER show a phone number, email, or website URL. The only interactive element on the ad page is the ScanLogo button. This is intentional — it keeps the design clean and forces one clear action.

Section 03

How It Works — The Exact User Flow

This is the confirmed step-by-step journey a customer goes through on NowQR:

1
Customer signs up & logs in
They create a free account on nowqr.com or scanlogos.com. The free tier lets them explore but nothing can be downloaded or published without payment. No tools are accessible without login.
🔐 Login required before any tool access
2
Choose a marketing goal (Call to Action)
They pick what they want people to DO when they scan or click. Options: Buy Now, Call Now, Give Now, Book Now, Watch Now, Order Now, or a custom action. This sets the tone for everything that follows.
🎯 This determines the page template + ScanLogo style suggestions
3
Enter business details → AI builds the ad page
Customer types in: business name, what they offer, who their audience is, and a short description. GPT-4o AI immediately generates a headline, sub-headline, description paragraph, and CTA button text. This content auto-fills into a mobile-optimized Canva template.
🤖 AI does the writing — customer just edits if they want to
4
Edit the ad page in the Canva-style editor
Customer sees their AI-generated page in a drag-and-drop editor. They can change colors, fonts, upload their logo or images, adjust text. What they CANNOT add: any URLs, phone numbers, or email addresses. The page is purely visual — the ScanLogo will be the only way to take action.
🎨 Design-only zone — no contact info, no external links
5
Get taken to the ScanLogo QR builder
Once the ad page is complete, the system automatically moves the customer to the QR creation step. This is a key moment — payment comes up here, not before. They haven't paid yet. They choose an animated ScanLogo style (spinning shield, rotating gear, pulsing eye, etc.) and enter their destination URL — the actual page they want people to land on (their store, PayPal, YouTube, booking page).
💳 Payment prompt appears at this step — not earlier
6
Customize the ScanLogo button
Customer picks their button style from pre-made animated options. They can: add their logo inside the QR center, change the button color, add their CTA text (e.g. "TAP TO ORDER"), and choose an animation (spin, pulse, expand). A short branded URL is auto-generated (e.g., nqr.ai/buy-abc123). A "Safe Scan" shield badge is shown for trust.
📲 Clickable on desktop + scannable on mobile ("Loaded QR")
7
Make payment & receive final deliverables
One-time payment (or credit deduction). After payment, the customer receives their complete package: the finished Canva ad page hosted on our subdomain, and the animated ScanLogo button as a downloadable file. The ScanLogo is already embedded into the ad page as the only clickable element.
🎁 Final product = Ad Page + ScanLogo Button, together
8
Use it — print it, post it, embed it anywhere
Customer downloads their ScanLogo as PNG, GIF, or WebP and places it on their physical flyers, social media posts, Canva designs, or any other material. When someone scans or clicks it, they are taken directly to the destination URL the customer set. Dynamic QR means the destination can be changed from the dashboard at any time — no reprint needed.
🔄 Dynamic QR: update destination URL anytime, code stays the same
9
Track results from the dashboard
Customer logs in anytime to see: how many people scanned, where they scanned from (location), what device they used, and what time of day. They can also update their destination URL, renew their ScanLogo, or create a new campaign.
📊 Real-time analytics + campaign management

Section 05

Domain & URL Structure

The client confirmed: "I want our new website only to use the namesake domains — nowqr.com, scanlogos.com — and we add extensions to the main domains."

📌 Decision: Subdomains (not long URL paths)

We use subdomains like give.nowqr.com rather than long paths like nowqr.com/give.user.html. Subdomains look more professional, are easier to remember, and work better in print. Laravel supports wildcard subdomain routing natively.

Section 06

Payment Model — One-Time or Credits (No Subscription)

The client specifically does not want monthly subscriptions. The model is either a one-time purchase or a credit-based system where customers buy credits and spend them per action.

Plan Cost What you get Type
Free $0 1 basic QR, watermarked exports, limited templates Free
Creator Pack $47 once 200 credits, all animated ScanLogos, dynamic QR, full editor One-Time
Agency Bundle $97 once 600 credits, everything + multi-campaign + priority support One-Time
Credit Top-Up Pay as needed Buy more credits when they run out Flexible
💡 Credit Cost Logic (Suggested)

Each action costs a small number of credits: Generating an AI page = 5 credits. Creating a ScanLogo = 3 credits. Updating a dynamic URL = 1 credit. Exporting social media sizes = 2 credits. This way customers buy once and use at their own pace.
